**TASK A2: Formulate the Equilibrium Equations**
*   **Effective Normal Base Force ($N'_i$):**
    $$N'_i = \frac{W_i + \lambda f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - \lambda f_i E_i - \frac{c' b_i \tan\alpha_i}{F}}{\cos\alpha_i + \frac{\sin\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{F}}$$
*   **Zhu et al. (2005) Explicit Equation for Factor of Safety (F):**
    $$F = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{c' b_i \sec\alpha_i + W_i \cos\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{m_{\alpha i} (1 + \lambda f_i A_i)} Q_{i+1} \right]}{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{W_i \sin\alpha_i}{m_{\alpha i} (1 + \lambda f_i A_i)} Q_{i+1} \right]}$$
    where $m_{\alpha i} = \cos\alpha_i + \frac{\sin\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{F}$, and $Q_{i+1} = \prod_{j=i+1}^n \Psi_j$
*   **Zhu et al. (2005) Explicit Equation for Scale Factor ($\lambda$):**
    $$\lambda = \frac{F \sum_{i=1}^n W_i \sin\alpha_i - \sum_{i=1}^n \left[ c' b_i \sec\alpha_i + \frac{W_i - \frac{c' b_i \tan\alpha_i}{F}}{m_{\alpha i}} \tan\phi' \right]}{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - f_i E_i}{m_{\alpha i}} \tan\phi' \right]}$$
*   **Transfer Coefficient ($\Psi_j$):**
    $$\Psi_j = \frac{1 + \lambda f_{j-1} A_j}{1 + \lambda f_j A_j} \quad \text{where} \quad A_j = \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}$$

**PART B — METHODOLOGICAL VERIFICATION**

**TASK B1: Angle Convention**
*   **Formula:** $\alpha_i = \arcsin\left(\frac{x_c - x_{mid,i}}{R}\right)$
*   **Sign Convention:** In the active zone ($x_{mid} < x_c$), the angle $\alpha$ evaluates to a **positive** sign (driving the mass downwards/forwards). In the passive zone ($x_{mid} > x_c$), $\alpha$ evaluates to a **negative** sign.

**TASK B2: Solver Strategy**
*   **Method Used:** Treated $F$ and $\lambda$ using the exact explicit recurrence relations derived by Zhu et al. (2005) integrated within a successive substitution fixed-point iteration loop, rather than passing them to a generic non-linear root finder like `scipy.optimize.fsolve`. 

**TASK B3: Base Normal Force**
*   **Formula:** $N'_i = \frac{W_i + \lambda f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - \lambda f_i E_i - \frac{c' b_i \tan\alpha_i}{F}}{\cos\alpha_i + \frac{\sin\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{F}}$
*   **Inclusion of Interslice Forces:** Yes, this formulation explicitly includes the interslice shear forces through the substitution $X = \lambda f(x) E$, appearing directly as the $(\lambda f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - \lambda f_i E_i)$ term.

**TASK B4: Transfer Coefficient ($\Psi$)**
*   **Exact Formula:**
    $$\Psi_j = \frac{1 + \lambda f_{j-1} \left( \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}} \right)}{1 + \lambda f_j \left( \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}} \right)}$$

**TASK B5: Interslice Function Indexing**
*   **Locations Evaluated:** For a given slice $k$, the interslice function $f(x)$ is evaluated strictly at the left boundary of the slice ($f_{k-1}$) and the right boundary of the slice ($f_k$).
*   **Multiplication:** They multiply the normal forces directly evaluated at those exact boundaries respectively, formulating the moment contribution term as: $(f_{k-1} E_{k-1} - f_k E_k)$.
