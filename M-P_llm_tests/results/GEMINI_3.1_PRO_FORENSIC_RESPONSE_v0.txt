    $$F = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{c' b_i \sec\alpha_i + W_i \cos\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{m_{\alpha i} (1 + \lambda f_i A_i)} Q_{i+1} \right]}{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{W_i \sin\alpha_i}{m_{\alpha i} (1 + \lambda f_i A_i)} Q_{i+1} \right]}$$

*   **Scale Factor ($\lambda$):**
    $$\lambda = \frac{F \sum_{i=1}^n W_i \sin\alpha_i - \sum_{i=1}^n \left[ c' b_i \sec\alpha_i + \frac{W_i - \frac{c' b_i \tan\alpha_i}{F}}{m_{\alpha i}} \tan\phi' \right]}{\sum_{i=1}^n \left[ \frac{f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - f_i E_i}{m_{\alpha i}} \tan\phi' \right]}$$
*   **Transfer Coefficient ($\Psi_j$):**
    $$\Psi_j = \frac{1 + \lambda f_{j-1} A_j}{1 + \lambda f_j A_j} \quad \text{where} \quad A_j = \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}$$

**TASK B3: Base Normal Force**
*   **Formula:** $N'_i = \frac{W_i + \lambda f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - \lambda f_i E_i - \frac{c' b_i \tan\alpha_i}{F}}{\cos\alpha_i + \frac{\sin\alpha_i \tan\phi'}{F}}$
*   **Inclusion:** Yes, it explicitly includes interslice normal forces ($E$) and interslice shear forces ($X$) substituted as $\lambda f_{i-1} E_{i-1} - \lambda f_i E_i$.

**TASK B4: Transfer Coefficient ($\Psi$)**
*   **Formula:** 
    $$\Psi_j = \frac{1 + \lambda f_{j-1} \left( \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}} \right)}{1 + \lambda f_j \left( \frac{\sin\alpha_j - \frac{\cos\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}}{\cos\alpha_j + \frac{\sin\alpha_j \tan\phi'}{F}} \right)}$$

**TASK B5: Interslice Function Indexing**
*   **Locations Evaluated:** Evaluated strictly at the left boundary of slice $k$ ($f_{k-1}$) and the right boundary of slice $k$ ($f_k$).
*   **Multiplication:** They multiply the normal forces directly at those exact boundaries as $(f_{k-1} E_{k-1} - f_k E_k)$.
