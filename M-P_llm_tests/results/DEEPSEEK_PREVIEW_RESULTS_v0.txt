**PART B — METHODOLOGICAL VERIFICATION**

**B1: Angle Convention**
\[
\alpha_i = \arctan\!\left(\frac{x_{\text{mid},i} - x_c}{y_c - y_{\text{bot},i}}\right)
\quad\text{with } (x_c,y_c)=(95,120).
\]
- Active zone (\(x_{\text{mid}} < 95\)): \(\alpha_i < 0\) (negative).
- Passive zone (\(x_{\text{mid}} > 95\)): \(\alpha_i > 0\) (positive).

**B2: Solver Strategy**
The explicit recurrence relations given in A2 were used. \(F\) was adjusted by a secant method on the force residual \(E_5(F,\lambda)=0\); \(\lambda\) was updated by a secant method on the moment residual \(\sum W_i(x_i-95) - R\sum S_i = 0\). No general-purpose non‑linear solver was employed.

**B3: Base Normal Force**
\[
N'_i = \frac{W_i + X_{i-1} - X_i + \dfrac{c' l_i}{F}\sin\alpha_i}
            {\cos\alpha_i - \dfrac{\tan\varphi'}{F}\sin\alpha_i},
\qquad
X_k = \lambda E_k.
\]
The formula explicitly includes the interslice shear forces \(X\) and, through the recurrence, the interslice normal forces \(E\).

**B4: Transfer Coefficient**
For a general interslice force function \(f(x)\),
\[
\Psi_{i-1} = \frac{ D_i - \lambda f_{i-1} K_i }{ D_i - \lambda f_i K_i }.
\]
With \(f(x)=1\) (Spencer assumption) this reduces to \(\Psi_{i-1} = 1\).

**B5: Interslice Function Indexing**
In the moment equilibrium equation used to update \(\lambda\), the function \(f(x)\) is evaluated at the **vertical boundaries of each slice**. For slice \(k\), the left boundary is at \(x_{k-1}\) and the right boundary at \(x_k\). The product \(f(x)\) multiplies the corresponding normal force \(E\):
\[
X_{k-1} = \lambda\, f(x_{k-1})\, E_{k-1}, \qquad
X_k     = \lambda\, f(x_k)\,     E_k.
\]
